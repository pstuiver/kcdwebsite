Alert program

The Alert Program was developed by Occupational Therapists to teach children self regulation and to teach adults how to support learning, attending and positive behaviour.

It uses the analogy that the body is like a car engine sometimes it runs too high, sometimes too low and sometimes just right.

Participation in the Alert Program will provide the child and adults with a common language and words to describe how their engine is running. It will also provide a range of simple, low-budget strategies and activities that can be incorporated easily into the home and into school routines in order to assist the child with feeling just right.

Elsje Stuiver Occupational Therapy has further enhanced the Alert Program by adding additional visual support and interactive cue cards to support children with special needs.
