Alert Program
 

The Alert Program was developed by Occupational Therapists to teach children self-regulation and to teach adults how to support learning, attending and positive behaviour in children.

It uses the analogy that the body is like a car engine sometimes running too high, sometimes too low and sometimes just right.

Participation in the Alert Program will provide the child and adults with a common language and words to describe how the child's engine is running.

ItÂ will also provide a range of simple, low-budget strategies and activities that can be incorporated easily into the home and into school routines, in order to assist the child with feeling just right.

Elsje Stuiver Occupational Therapy has enhanced the Alert Program by adding additional visual support and interactive cue cards, to support children with special needs.
